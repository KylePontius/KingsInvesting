Overview

Sea Limited is an incredibly diverse company that is a prominent player in three main sectors, e-commerce (Shopee), digital entertainment (Garena), and digital financial services (Sea Money). The company is based in Singapore and was created in 2009 with Garena was it's only product at the time until Shopee in 2015, and Sea Money in 2014.

Fundamental Analysis

Despite missing on earnings expectations in recent years, it simply does not matter at this point in the company. Sea Limited is only getting started with its business expenditures, expanding and reinvesting every chance they can get to achieve a higher market share and user base in its product areas. However, their revenue has been and is expected to keep on rising at exponential rates as a result of their continuous instances of expansion and investment, which is in turn causing them to fall short on EPS in the short term.

Simple Technical Analysis

Sea Limited stock has broken support levels and has been met with low interest as seen in the drastic drop in RSI since it's all-time highs.

Advanced Technical Analysis

A clear 5 wave structure has completed on the 2 7ear daily chart, calling for an a-b-c movement before a new trend is established. This occurred after hitting the 161.8% extension, and may have just completed an "a" wave at the 78.6% extension, where a corrective jump may follow to give way to lower-lows.

Thesis

Sea Limited is given a rank of 6 or "hold" for various reasons. The company surely has fundamentals available to set it up as a thriving company in the coming years (seen through its expansions and worthy investments), but as of now the technicals simply are not favorable. A standard 5 wave completion can be seen on the 1 year daily chart, thus insinuating an a-b-c down before a new trend can be established. There is no point in catching a falling knife, a bottom and new trend must be established, as charts on different time frames are not as clear to indicate a continuation of the bullish trend.
