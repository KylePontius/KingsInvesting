About

King's Investing is a new Georgia-based investment research company that provides its ratings of securities to anyone through the use of research to provide customers with adequate information on companies to make their own decisions. All fourty-seven of our analysts have at least five years of experience from companies such as Bank of America, Morgan Stanley, Charles Schwab, amongst others. In terms of research and types of investing, King's Investing focuses on various sectors and methods, ensuring every customer has some extent to their preferred sector.
