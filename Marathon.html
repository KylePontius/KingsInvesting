Overview

Marathon Digital Holdings is a blockchain company with a focus on mining and holding cryptocurrency, primarily Bitcoin and some Ethereum. The company was formed in 2010 and is based in Las Vegas, Nevada.

Fundamental Analysis

Although MARA has had very recent hardships with stock offerings and the volatility of Bitcoin, we are under the belief that Marathon is still in a prime position to thrive in the coming months and years. The growing adoption of Bitcoin by established entities and growing user adoption of Bitcoin is allowing the price of Bitcoin to grow, thus putting MARA in a better financial situation, as they are a proxy version of Bitcoin.

Simple Technical Analysis

MARA has recently broken it's resistance line and reenter the support-resistance channel, the RSI is in the midst of a consolidation period, indicating a move in between the channel for a certain period until Bitcoin's path is present.

Advanced Technical Analysis

Marathon has just completed a wave 3 of 5 on the 1 year daily chart, with a bottom of wave 4 being probable. The next most likely target is around $123, or the 261.8% extension.

Thesis

MARA is given a rating of 7, or buy, for various fundamental and technical reasons. Fundamentally, their holding and mining of Bitcoin has proven to be incredibly lucrative despite drawbacks in the asset, Bitcoin is believed to rise in the coming years, putting MARA in a better position. Furthermore, the indication of a bottom in wave 4 gives a possible entry for the stock to more than double in value in the coming months. However, MARA is not given a higher rating due to the risk revolving around the asset.
