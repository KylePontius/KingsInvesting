Overview

The VanEck Vectors Semiconductor etf is a group of holdings with an emphasis on semiconductor/chip-based companies. The performance of SMH is meant to track the top 25 semiconductor companies according to the MVIS US Listed Semiconductor Index.

Fundamental Analysis

SMH focuses on a lucrative niche which has not yet been able to maximize profits, thus presenting a very attractive entry at any price point in the near future. The current macroeconomic situation for the semiconductor sector is nowhere near the capacity of production it should be/wants to be, demand for chips has been higher than ever, and the inability to pump out these chips is leading to a drastic price in their products. If and when the holdings are capable of pushing out the desired product, their sales will increase along with the opportunities as they can further establish themselves to more customers. Only one of the holdings is unfavorable, being Intel, who has sufferred both fundamentally and technically compared to their peers significantly.

Simple Technical Analysis

SMH has broken both resistance lines on the 6 month daily chart and 1 year daily chart, insinuating a drop back into the area between the support and resistance lines. The RSI is quite volatile, however if current levels are maintained the price of SMH is still set to grow.

Advanced Technical Analysis

SMH has just completed a wave 3 of 5 on the one year daily chart, indicating the beginning of a wave 4 of 5, with a possible pullback to the 161.8% retracement before the start of wave 5, which may reach the 423.6% extension if the proceeding structure proves to be standard and not a diagnol of sorts.

Thesis

SMH is given a rating of 9/10 for various fundamental and technical reasons. All of the holdings of SMH are not anywhere near their full potential due to the current macroeconomic situation. Only one of the holdings yields valid concern for the ETF, being Intel, however the amount of Intel in SMH is outweighed by the top prospects in the semiconductor industry. Technical reasons are favorable for an entry in SMH as despite being in the middle of a wave 4, the upside is almost guaranteed to be immense, no matter the entry point before the wave 5 confirmation, as the diagnols are not currently probable to occur.
