Our Research

Here at King's Investing, we are fully aware that this service will be used by traders/investors at various levels in terms of skill, knowledge, and areas of expertise, which is why we have specifically formatted every piece of analysis to ensure adequate information on every security. However, there are areas in which we exceed, and ones in which we fall short in. At King's Investing, we specifically excel in industrials, information technology, energy, materials, and financials. However, we do not perform exceptionally in consumer discretionary, consumer staples, health care, real estate, utilities, and communication services. However, in each sector of the market we ensure to beat the average growth of the S&P 500 every year.
A list of our means of technical analysis and fundamental analysis are provided below:

Fundamental Analysis:

With the King's Investing service, members are provided with ratings on each security in terms of their dividend, risk, profitability, along with our analyst and quantitative algorithm ratings.

Rating Criterias:
  • Dividend - Dividend payment compared to peers in industry, along with payment consistency
  • Risk - Average of analyst and quantitative odds of loss when entering position, relative to a short or buy rating
  • Profitability - Average of analyst and quantitative odds of gains when entering position, relative to a short or buy rating
  • Analyst - Likeness or distaste of a security by a professional King's Investing analyst
  • Quantitative - Likeness or distate of a security based upon volume, historical data, moving averages, and MACD
