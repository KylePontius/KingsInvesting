Overview

QQQ is one of the most popular exchange traded funds in the world, the Invesco ETF is meant to track the progress of the top 100 Nasdaq companies, including: Microsoft, Apple, Tesla, Alphabet, amongst other prestigous companies.

Fundamental Analysis

The holdings of QQQ are nothing short of being exemplary companies in the market, each of its holdings have incredible potential for growth in the coming years. Furthermore, the ETF is actively managed by the reputable investment management firm Invesco, which will make adjustments to the holdings if needed.

Simple Technical Analysis

QQQ is far from its support and resistance lines, indicating a possible move up towards upper resistance after a further approach towards it's support. The RSI is quite volatile, however new highs are consistenly set.

Advanced Technical Analysis

It is apparent one the one year daily chart, that QQQ has not completed a proper five waves, in fact, it can be argued that the current position is a 4 of 3 of 5, indicating that QQQ can be subject to a much higher bull run due to the nature of the MACD and marginal run up of wave 3 of 3 of 5.

Thesis

QQQ is given a rating of 9, or accumulate, as the holdings within the fund are both fundametnally and technically well, paving the way for a higher run up after the wave 4 of 3 completes, however, once an impulsive five wave structure once a wave 4 of 3 completes, wave 3 of 5 will be over, and wave 4 of 5 will begin, signaling a much more grand pullback for early 2022.
